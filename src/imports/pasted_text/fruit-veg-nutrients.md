KEY FOR TAGS
Fibre scale: Very low · Low · Medium · High · Very high
GI bands: Low GI (0–55) · Medium GI (56–69) · High GI (70+)
Inhibitor tags: Oxalates (inhibitor) · Phytates (inhibitor) · Tannins (inhibitor)
Potentiator notes: ⚡ tag marks ingredients that significantly boost absorption of another
Warning tags: ⚠️ marks interactions worth flagging (drug interactions, low nutritional density)
❄️ Freeze-Enhanced: anthocyanin or enzyme availability increased by freezing

A few notes on the corrections made: Frozen Banana had "Polyphenol Oxidase" listed as a tag, which is actually an enzyme that causes browning — it's an interaction flag rather than a nutrient, so I replaced it with the actual nutritional profile and noted Resistant Starch (which increases when bananas are frozen). Spirulina's B12 tag has been corrected with a note that it's an analogue and not bioavailable in humans — this matters for your scoring engine as it's a common misconception. Turmeric now correctly flags that it requires both dietary fat and black pepper for curcumin absorption, which is a strong potentiator relationship for your interaction matrix. Grapefruit has a drug interaction flag added as it inhibits CYP3A4 enzymes and can affect medications — important for a health app. Rice Milk now carries a low nutritional density warning.
